1
Rated questions show 5 large numbered buttons (1 to 5) instead of a post-it zone.

Click your score. The button turns orange to confirm your selection.

Click Submit Rating. Your vote is counted and a bar chart updates instantly.
4
You can only rate each question once. The average score and distribution are shown to everyone.

3
